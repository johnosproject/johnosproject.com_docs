# JCP Front End Login and out user

import YoutubeVideo from '../../../../../_video_youtube.mdx'

To login to the JCP Front End:
1. open the [JCP front End](https:://www.johnosproject.org/frontend) home page
1. click on ```Login``` link in the User's menu on top-right of the page
1. fill the login form with user's credentials and proceed

To login to the JCP Front End:
1. open the [JCP front End](https:://www.johnosproject.org/frontend) home page
1. click on ```Logout``` link in the User's menu on top-right of the page
