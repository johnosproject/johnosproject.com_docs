# JCP Front End Create a new user account

import YoutubeVideo from '../../../../../_video_youtube.mdx'

To create a new account you must
1. open the [JCP front End](https:://www.johnosproject.org/frontend) home page
1. click on ```Register``` link in the User's menu on top-right of the page
1. fill the registration form with your info and proceed
1. validate your email address by click on received email
1. grant required scopes to JCP Front End service
